Example Queries for Smart GECI

The natural language query system allows users to ask questions about infrastructure and spatial information.

Common example queries include:

Where is the CSE block?
Where is the library building?
How many floors does the administrative building have?
Show all academic buildings.
Show the campus infrastructure map.
What facilities are available in the mechanical block?

Users may also ask descriptive questions about the system itself.

Examples include:

What is Smart GECI?
How does the system work?
What features does the system provide?

Environmental queries may include:

What is the Urban Heat Island effect?
Show temperature distribution in the study area.

These example queries demonstrate how the system allows users to interact with infrastructure data through simple natural language questions.
